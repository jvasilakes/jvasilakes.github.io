iDISK: the Integrated Dietary Supplements Knowledge Base
Developed at the University of Minnesota Institute for Health Informatics, iDISK is a standardardized representation of a large number of dietary supplements that links to the Unified Medical Language System (UMLS) and MedDRA. It is built semi-automatically by normalizing and integrated information from various resources.
