About

I am currently working on my MSc thesis at the University of Edinburgh. For my project I am adding an open-domain semantic parser to Python's NLTK library. As of now, I'm using CCG and Neo-Davidsonian event semantics.

I completed my BA in Philosophy at Loyola University of Chicago. My honors thesis was titled "The Word of Speech". In it I discussed the theory of speech introduced by Merleau-Ponty in which the speech act is analogous to bodily interaction with the world.

I am also interested in language in general, Dante, Italian comics, classical Greek, all sorts of music, and travelling as much as possible.
